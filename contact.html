Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the things people ask most.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in? ▼

Walk-ins are welcome for quick questions and diagnostics, but we recommend booking an appointment for repairs and custom builds to guarantee a time slot.

Can I bring my own parts for a custom build? ▼

Absolutely! You can bring your own parts, buy through us, or mix and match. We'll help with compatibility checks either way.

How long does a custom build take? ▼

Most builds are completed within 2–3 business days once all parts arrive. Rush builds (24-hour turnaround) are available for an additional fee.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs? ▼

Yes — all labour comes with a 30-day warranty. Parts carry the manufacturer's warranty, and we'll handle the RMA process for you if needed.
